Quick Links

Some pages on the site can be found by holding the "command" key (Mac) or "Ctrl" key (Windows) and typing a letter on your keyboard (accesskeys). On the sites Home Page you can go straight to the registration page by typing "n" for "New Account".

Some acceskeys can be used on any page on the site:

  • "h" for "Home" for the sites home
  • "a" for "Account" for your account homepage
  • "r" for "Rules" for the rules page
  • "t" for "Terms of Service" for the Terms of Service page

Note: Some computers may have these letters set as keyboard shortcuts and therefore may not be able to be used for site navigation.
